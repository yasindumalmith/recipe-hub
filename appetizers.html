Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400째F (200째C). Place baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spoon the tomato mixture onto toasted baguette slices and serve immediately.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C). Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
  2. Mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan, cream cheese, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Fill mushroom caps with the mixture and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes until golden and tender. Serve warm.
