Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate and let cool slightly.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale. Stir in melted chocolate and vanilla.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. In a separate bowl, whip cream until soft peaks form.
  4. Fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture, then gently fold in egg whites.
  5. Spoon into serving dishes and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple, diced
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 2 kiwis, peeled and sliced
  • 1 orange, segmented
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Combine all fruit in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, mix honey and lemon juice.
  3. Pour dressing over fruit and toss gently to combine.
  4. Chill before serving.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until smooth. Stir in milk.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. In a large bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the cooled egg mixture into the whipped cream.
  4. In a shallow dish, combine coffee and coffee liqueur. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are soaked but not soggy.
  5. Arrange half of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish. Spread half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  7. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder.

Lemon Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, powdered sugar, and salt. Cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in egg yolk and mix until the dough comes together. Press the dough into the bottom and up the sides of a tart pan.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together lemon juice, granulated sugar, eggs, and lemon zest.
  5. Pour the lemon mixture into the tart crust.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is lightly golden.
  7. Let cool, then dust with powdered sugar before serving.
